## 1. Assessment Instructions

Welcome to the Module 8 Assessment for **Full Stack .NET Development with C#, ASP.NET Core, and Angular**. This assessment is designed to test your understanding of Angular Routing and Navigation concepts covered throughout this module.

  * **Topics Covered:** Client-side routing, setting up `AppRoutingModule`, defining routes with `path` and `component`, the `` directive, navigating with `routerLink` and the `Router` service, route parameters, query parameters, fragments, child routes, lazy loading, and route guards (`CanActivate`, `CanDeactivate`, `CanLoad`, `Resolve`).
  * **Assessment Format:** This assessment consists of 12 questions, including a mix of single-choice and multiple-choice formats.
  * **Tips for Success:**
    * Read each question carefully before answering.
    * Pay close attention to the details in the code snippets and scenarios presented.
    * Consider all options, especially for multiple-choice questions where more than one answer might be correct.
    * Review the module content if you are unsure about any topic.



Good luck!
